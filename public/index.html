Rules

  • Each player will be given seven cards at the start of the game.
  • The game will start with one card facing up on the table.
  • Everyone will get one chance to place a card with either a matching number or color, if they do not have a card they will need to draw a new card until they can play a card on the table.
  • When a player has run out of cards they will win the game.
Eins is an interactive online card game based off the classic Uno card game. The game starts after four people have joined the game and finishes when one of the players have run out of cards in their deck. Please feel free to play a game, or you can check the scores and rules on the top navbar. Enjoy and have fun!

About Us

This site was designed in Irvine, CA and is based off the Uno card game. Using web development tools such as the MEAN development stack, and socket.io we were able to create a modern website that has the capability of playing a game with real time updating from the back-end node.js server. If you have any questions, concerns or advice on how to make this site better you are more then welcome to send us a message. We are looking forward to your input and hope you enjoy the game!
